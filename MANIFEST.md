## Provenance

All source texts were downloaded from Project Gutenberg (https://www.gutenberg.org/), a free online library of public domain books. Texts were retrieved as UTF-8 plaintext files using the Gutenberg cache URL pattern: `https://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/{gid}/pg{gid}.txt`. Downloads were performed in April 2026.

Source files are archived in `source_texts.zip` and hosted on UVA Box at: https://virginia.box.com/s/n9ojz511cikug8db50y2pkw74522cvdw

## Description

The corpus consists of 12 English-language novels spanning two literary genres — Gothic fiction and early science fiction — published between 1764 and 1912. The corpus was selected to enable comparative analysis of emotional, thematic, and lexical patterns across these two related but distinct literary traditions.

## Format

- File format: Plain text (UTF-8 encoding)
- File naming: `{gutenberg_id}.txt` (e.g., `84.txt` for Frankenstein)
- Internal structure: Unstructured prose. Gutenberg boilerplate headers and footers were stripped during processing (see `01_acquire_corpus.ipynb`). The raw downloaded files with boilerplate intact are preserved in the `source_texts/` directory.
